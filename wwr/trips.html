Rapid Rush Day Trip

Build-Your-Own Adventure

Perfect for thrill-seekers with limited time, this one-day experience takes you down some of the most exciting rapids in the region, led by experienced guides. After an exhilarating morning on the water, enjoy a riverside lunch before an optional guided nature walk to learn about local wildlife and scenery.

This package is designed to suit your unique interests and schedule. Choose your adventure level—from intense, multi-day rafting excursions to relaxed days filled with fishing and nature walks. Add activities like guided wildlife tours or scenic hikes, and customize the trip length to match your perfect getaway. Our team works closely with you to craft an itinerary that delivers an unforgettable outdoor experience tailored just for you.

Weekend Adventure Camp

Ideal for those who want to pack a weekend full of excitement and relaxation. Start with a thrilling rafting expedition, followed by an evening at our scenic campsite, complete with campfire dinner and stargazing. The next morning, enjoy a peaceful guided fishing trip to wind down.

Ultimate Wilderness Expedition

This all-inclusive, multi-day adventure is designed for true outdoor enthusiasts. With multiple rafting excursions, guided hikes, nights under the stars, and daily fishing opportunities, this trip lets you experience the best of nature from thrilling rapids to serene mornings on the water.
