Santiago

Santiago, the vibrant capital of Chile, is nestled in a valley surrounded by the majestic Andes. This bustling metropolis seamlessly blends modernity with history, showcasing sleek skyscrapers alongside colonial architecture. With a diverse culinary scene and easy access to both mountains and the Pacific coast, Santiago is a captivating city that inspires exploration. I love waking up and seeing the beautiful Andes mountains from my window that's different every day.
